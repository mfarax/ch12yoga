Path of Light Yoga Studio

Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minutes class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minutes class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. this calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.

Relax Anytime with Savasana

"Prepare yourself for savasana. Lie down on your yoga mat with your arms at your side with palms up. Close your eyes and breath slowly but deeply. Sink into the mat and your worries slip away. When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself to a setting position with crossed legs. Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life. Namaste"
